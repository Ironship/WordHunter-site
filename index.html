Word Hunter 1.0.4 for desktop and Android

Read real texts. Turn every unknown word into a review.

Word Hunter is a local-first reader, vocabulary trainer, PDF/OCR workspace, and SRS review tool for learners who want to study from real books, articles, subtitles, and scanned documents.
